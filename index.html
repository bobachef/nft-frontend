Innovations and Improvements

CryptoPunks (2017) is one of the first NFTs and helped inspire the ERC-721 standard.

  • Upgraded from pixel art to hand drawn original anime art.
  • Each attribute was custom made for each base.
  • Crafted a unique look and feel for each base type.
  • Created 50+ extra attributes using existing attributes that weren’t utilized on certain character types adding millions of new combinations.
  • Example: Hoodie Attribute. Original CryptoPunks only gave this attribute to male and zombies. We’ve expanded it to include all of the other types of characters as well. The female hoodie was also updated to give her a little red riding hood look.
